Humidity vs Latitude

Description

We see in this plot that cloudiness is less dependent on latitude than temperature. This is evident by looking at cities with 100% humidity. These cities span from below -40 latitude to a little below 80 latitude. Further statistical analysis is required to discover any less apparent trends.
