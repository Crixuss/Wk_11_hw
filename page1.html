Summary: Max Temp vs Latitude

Welcome to the weather dashboard. This dashboard illustrates the effects that latitude has on temperatue, cloudiness, humidity, and wind speed. Randomly selected cities were used to supply the data. The temperature scatterplot shows that the closer you are to the equator, the higher the observed temp.
